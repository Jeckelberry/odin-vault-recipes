Jelly Meatballs

Tasty grape jelly and bbq sauce meatball appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 32oz grape jelly
  • 24 oz bbq sauce of your choosing
  • 6lb of frosen cocktail-meatballs

Cooking Steps

  1. Combine jelly and bbq sauce in a bowl and mix together.
  2. Place meatballs inside slow cooker and cover with sauce mixture.
  3. Cook on low for 3-4 hours
